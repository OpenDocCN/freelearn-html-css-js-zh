Summary

We have provided some important plugins that may be useful in a real fully-fledged app. This is not a comprehensive list of plugins as it's not possible to provide the entire list in this appendix. You can search at https://build.phonegap.com/plugins or http://www.plugreg.com for more plugins and descriptions.
